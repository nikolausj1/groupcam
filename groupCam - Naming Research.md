# groupCam - Naming Research Longlist

This is a preliminary naming screen, not trademark clearance. It contains the original 64 candidates plus 70 additional researched candidates, for a total of 134.

Screening used Apple’s U.S. software-search results, exact-name web searches focused on photography/software, and Verisign `.com` RDAP on July 13, 2026. “No record” means the registry returned no registration record at that moment; it is not a guarantee that a domain is purchasable. A finalist still needs professional federal/state/common-law trademark review, social-handle checks, and international review before public branding.

## Current decision

Justin selected **groupCam** as the current name on July 13, 2026. Use it throughout the prototype and PRD. Before public distribution, confirm that the name can be reserved in App Store Connect and obtain formal trademark/common-law clearance because of the legacy same-field GroupCam use, registered `groupcam.com`, and weak descriptiveness. Do not rename the product unless Justin directs it or clearance blocks the name.

Current positioning direction:

- **Name:** groupCam
- **Subtitle candidate:** Everyone in the picture
- **Campaign line:** Two photos. Everyone in.
- **Domain fallback signals:** `getgroupcam.com`, `groupcamapp.com`, and `trygroupcam.com` showed no Verisign record during screening; none is reserved or owned.

## Focused territory: Group Cam / Group Camera

Researched July 13, 2026.

### Verdict

- **GroupCam / Group Cam:** not recommended as the primary public brand. No exact current U.S. App Store title appeared in the official search, but a Copenhagen group-photo-sharing startup previously used the exact `GroupCam` name in the same software/category territory. `groupcam.com` is registered. Even if the legacy company is no longer active, the prior same-field use, registered exact domain, generic construction, and weak search ownership create avoidable confusion.
- **Group Camera:** viable if immediate clarity matters more than trademark strength. No exact current U.S. App Store title appeared, and `groupcamera.com` returned no Verisign registration record during screening. However, the phrase is highly descriptive, appears within the active [Realty ONE Group Camera](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/realty-one-group-camera/id1496650264) title, and is widely used to describe shared/event-camera products. It would be difficult to protect or dominate in search.
- **Strategic recommendation:** use **Group Camera** as an App Store subtitle/category phrase beneath a more distinctive brand, for example `[Brand] - Group Camera` or subtitle `Group photos with everyone in`. If Justin deliberately prefers a plain descriptive public name, **Group Camera** is the strongest available option in this territory, subject to rechecking the App Store, domain, and legal clearance immediately before adoption.
